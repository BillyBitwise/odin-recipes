Banana Cake

Ingredients

  • 165 grams of all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of cinnamon
  • 2 tsp of cocoa
  • 1 banana
  • 95 grams of sugar
  • 125 ml of milk
  • 1 tbsp of oil
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Read through all the Ingredients and Instructions to ensure you have everything you need
  2. With a scale, measure 165 grams of all purpose flour and add it to a big bowl
  3. Measure and add a teaspoon of baking soda to the bowl
  4. Measure and add 2 teaspoons of cocoa to the bowl
  5. Measure and add half a teaspoon of cinnamon to the bowl
  6. Mix the contents of the bowl gently with a wisk and set aside
  7. Using a new bowl, chop a banana into thin slices and mash to a paste
  8. Measure and add 95 grams of sugar to the banana paste
  9. Using a measing cup, add 125 millilitres of milk to the banana paste mixture
  10. Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil aswell as 1 egg to the banana paste mixture
  11. Using a wisk, stir the banana paste mixture for a lighter texture
  12. Recall our dry ingredients bowl.
  13. Gradually stir and wisk the dry mix into the banana paste mixture until thick like pancake mix
  14. Line a (20cm x 20cm x 5cm) baking tray with baking paper along all surfaces of the tray
  15. Pour the banana paste mixture onto the baking paper in the tray
  16. Place the tray in an oven pre-heated to 350 degrees fahrenheit for 30 minutes
  17. Remove from oven and let sit for 10 minutes.
  18. Remove cake from tray and baking paper and allow to sit until cool
  19. Wrap the cake and store in fridge to prevent cake from drying
