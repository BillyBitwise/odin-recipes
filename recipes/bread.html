Bread

Description

Make your own bread from home. It's easy and fun. You probably have all the utensils needed.

Active quick rise yeast may be the only thing you really need to make four wholesome Kaisers.

Bread

Ingredients

  • 235 grams of all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp of active quick rise yeast
  • 1/2 tsp of sugar
  • 7/8 tsp of salt
  • 20 grams of vegetable oil
  • 145 grams of warm water

Instructions

  1. Read through all the Ingredients and Instructions to ensure you have everything you need
  2. With a scale, measure 235 grams of all purpose flour and add it to a big bowl
  3. Measure and add a half teaspoon of sugar to the bowl
  4. Measure and add a teaspoon of active quick rise yeast to the bowl
  5. Approximate 7/8 of a teaspoon of salt, and add to the bowl
  6. Mix the contents of the bowl gently with a wisk and set aside
  7. With a scale, measure 145 grams of warm water in a (measuring) cup
  8. Measure and add 20 grams of vegetable oil to the cup
  9. Gradually pour the contents of the cup into the bowl you set aside
  10. Mix the liquids together with the dry ingredients using a wooden spoon
  11. Complete the mixing by hand if need be to produce a moist ball of dough
  12. Knead the dough for 10 minutes adding flour if moist, or water if dry
  13. Cover the dough with a bowl and let sit for 10 minutes
  14. Cut and roll the dough into 4 equal sized balls
  15. Place baking paper on baking tray. Place balls of dough apart from each other on tray
  16. Cover the dough with a light cloth and allow to rise one hour
  17. Lightly brush warm water on each ball of dough and place into oven at 375 degrees Fahrenheit for 20 minutes
  18. Remove from oven and cover the bread with baking paper. Allow to cool for one hour
